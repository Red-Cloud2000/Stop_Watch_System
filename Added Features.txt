#Added Features:
----------------
a- 2 push buttons for time editing:
    . one to increment minutes by 1
    . one to increment hours by 1

b- 2 debugging push buttons for better testing and less wasted time:
    . one to make minutes be 59
    . one to make seconds be 58

c- 7-segment blinks 10 times every hour

d- After each 24hours the stopwatch resets.
